I am currently a undergraduate student at the **University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)**. 

My research interests lie at the intersection of **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and **Distributed Machine Learning**, with a particular focus on:
* **LLM-based Agents & Safety**: Developing rational agent frameworks for text anonymization, privacy preservation, and adversarial defense.
* **Robust Federated Learning**: Designing secure and fair decentralized training mechanisms.

I am always open to academic collaborations and discussions. Please feel free to reach out to me via email.
